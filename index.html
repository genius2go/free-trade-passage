⚖️ Outside via Cape Henry (preferred) or inside via Chesapeake City / C&D Canal
▶ Outside — exit Cape Henry

Exit Hampton Roads via Cape Henry, run NE up the Delmarva coast, enter Cape May Inlet from the ocean on the east side. ~185 nm, comfortable full day. SW or W winds under 15 kts, seas under 4 ft.

▶ Inside — Bay / Chesapeake City / C&D Canal (if offshore rough)

North up the Chesapeake Bay to Chesapeake City (~100 nm), through the C&D Canal into Delaware Bay, run south to Cape May and enter from the bay/canal side. Longer but fully protected.
